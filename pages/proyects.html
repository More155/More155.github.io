Guess The Word

A fun and challenging word-guessing game built with Js. You have a limited number of attempts to guess the secret word letter by letter. To help you out, the game provides helpful hints for each word if you find it too difficult. There are 10 unique words to guess across multiple rounds, making it perfect for a quick brain-teasing session.

Test your vocabulary and deduction skills—will you guess the word before running out of attempts?
